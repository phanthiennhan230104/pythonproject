E-LEARNING

The online learning system is designed to help students access knowledge flexibly, anytime and anywhere. This allows students to take initiative in their studies and improve their programming skills.

Learning

Lecture content is organized by topic, allowing students to easily follow, study, and review knowledge.

Practice

A variety of practical exercises help reinforce theoretical knowledge and enhance problem-solving skills.

AI-Generated Practice Quizzes

Automatically generate customized practice quizzes using AI based on course content or user input. This feature helps students reinforce knowledge, identify weaknesses, and prepare for exams effectively.

Online IDE

Allows students to write, run, and test code directly on the platform without needing to install any software.
